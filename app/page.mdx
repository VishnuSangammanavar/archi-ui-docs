### What is Archi-UI?

**Archi-UI** is a thoughtfully crafted collection of UI components designed to streamline the development of modern web applications. We focus on providing:

* **Robust & Reliable Components:** Battle-tested and production-ready components that just work.
* **Accessibility by Design:** Every component is built with WCAG guidelines in mind, ensuring your applications are inclusive.
* **Exceptional Developer Experience:** Clear APIs, comprehensive documentation, and flexible customization options.
* **Performance Optimized:** Components are engineered for efficiency, contributing to fast-loading and smooth user experiences.
* **Consistent Design Language:** A unified aesthetic ensures your applications look polished and professional.

### Why Documentation Matters
